Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Alternatively, you can use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all packaging and tags attached. Some items like personalized products or intimate apparel may not be eligible for return. Please visit our Returns & Refunds page for more detailed information.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes that may apply when receiving packages internationally.
